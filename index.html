vinFinder

This is a tool for inputting a vin number of a vehicle against a database to verify if the vehicle has raced in prestigious races in the past.

Problem: The business owner needed a way to instantaneously identifying the history of a vehicle, while standing in someone's garage or barn, by its vin number.

Solution: This app allowed for quick and constant access to an cloud-based database of records regarding cars with race history.

Due to the proprietary nature of this project and the data included, I can not show screenshots of the project. The functionality is such that a vin number, or a portion of a vin number, can be entered in the app, and if the vehicle raced a prestigious race data regarding the race history displays on screen.
