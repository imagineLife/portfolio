vinFinder

This is a tool for checking a vin number of a vehicle against a database to verify if the vehicle has raced in prestigious races in the past. This tool met a need for instantly identifying a car that raced a race but the current owner may not have knowledge of the vehicles history.

Due to the proprietary nature of this project and the data included, I can not show screenshots of the project. The functionality is such that a vin number, or a portion of a vin number, can be entered in the app, and if the vehicle raced a prestigious race data regarding the race history displays on screen.
